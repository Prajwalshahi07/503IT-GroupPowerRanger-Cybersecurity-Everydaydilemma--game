Front page (Welcome page of prototype)
Our front page will be slightly decorated and will have the logo of our game and also the title which is “Everyday Dilemma”
Then there will be three buttons in the front page under the title of the game. The buttons are HOW TO PLAY, PLAY and SCORES.
These buttons will be neon coloured which is a part of our colour theme. Clicking on each button will lead to a new page.
HOW TO PLAY buttons consists of all the instructions that will help the user understand the game.
About “HOW TO PLAY” page
First there will be objective of the game stated which is to identify phising mails and texts. Then proceed to choose the safest option.
Then points  about how the  game works
1)We have three scenarios set up where the user will be playing as a student of Coventry university.
2)They will be given examples of email and text received by the student that is the user and they have to identify whether the given mail is a scam or a legit email.
3)The user will have a limited time frame to answer the question. There will be minimum three options for the user. Two of the options carry varying  points according to safety level. The wrong option will lead to deduction of points.
4)After clicking an option  there will automatically pop up that gives feedback on your answer and you will also get learning objectives according your answer and how u can improve your knowledge.
5)According to the question you choose you will go to different steps and levels of question and you go on answering.
6)If the user ends up choosing the wrong answer their details will be hacked by the attacker so the user will not pass the semester safely.
But if they do select correct answers or more suitable answers they will pass the semester safely.
And that is how the game ends. The user can always replay.
