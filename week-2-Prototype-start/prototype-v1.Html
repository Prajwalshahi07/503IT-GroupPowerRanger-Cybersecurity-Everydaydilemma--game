HOW TO PLAY

Step 1: Read the Scenario Carefully
There are 3 real-life scenarios (Email & SMS) that Coventry University students commonly receive.
Step 2: Choose the Best Action
You will be given 3 options. Choose the safest action to avoid the phishing attack.
Step 3: Earn Points + Get Feedback
• +25 points for the correct/safe choice
• -5 to -25 points for risky or wrong choices
You will receive instant feedback explaining the phishing red flags.
Step 4: Learn & Level Up
Master the 3 key phishing red flags and improve your “Semester Health” score.
Complete all missions to earn your Phishing Defender Certificate.
